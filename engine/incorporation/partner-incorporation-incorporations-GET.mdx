---
title: "List incorporations"
openapi: "GET /partner/incorporation/companies/{companyId}/incorporations"
---

Lists incorporations for a company accessible to your partner account.

Use `limit` and `offset` for pagination. The `count` field is the total number of matching incorporations before pagination.

## Incorporation status

Each returned incorporation includes `incorporationStatus`:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `submitted` | Commenda has received the incorporation request. |
| `in_progress` | Commenda is actively processing the incorporation. |
| `blocked` | The incorporation needs additional information or resolution before it can continue. |
| `completed` | The incorporation has been completed. |
