---
title: "Link a participant document"
openapi: "POST /partner/incorporation/{incorporationId}/documents"
---

Links a registered incorporation file to a participant as a typed document.

Files are the low-level storage object. Documents are the incorporation requirement being satisfied, such as `passport_scan` or `utility_bill`.

The `fileId` must first be registered to this incorporation with `POST /partner/incorporation/{incorporationId}/files`.

```json
{
  "participantId": "participant_123",
  "documentKind": "passport_scan",
  "fileId": 456
}
```

For Singapore private limited companies, each individual director and individual shareholder needs both `passport_scan` and `utility_bill` documents. One document kind does not satisfy the other.
