---
title: "Register an incorporation participant"
openapi: "POST /partner/incorporation/{incorporationId}/participants"
---

Registers a reusable Commenda OS key person or business entity as a participant in an incorporation.

Participants are the incorporation-specific layer that assigns roles such as `director` and `shareholder`. The underlying person or business entity remains reusable across Commenda OS and can be used by future KYC, e-signature, and document-review workflows.

KYC checks, e-signature execution, and document review are not performed by this endpoint in the MVP.

```json
{
  "participantType": "individual",
  "resource": {
    "resourceType": "keyPerson",
    "resourceId": "12"
  },
  "roles": [
    {
      "role": "director"
    },
    {
      "role": "shareholder",
      "ownershipPercentage": 100
    }
  ]
}
```

For Singapore private limited companies, individual participants with a `director` or `shareholder` role require participant documents such as `passport_scan` and `utility_bill`.
