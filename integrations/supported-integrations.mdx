Commenda integrates with accounting software, e-commerce platforms, payment processors, and bank accounts to automatically sync your financial data.

## Accounting and finance

| Platform | Syncing | Tax calculation | Notes |
|----------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| **QuickBooks** | Supported | Supported | Full transaction and invoice syncing |
| **Xero** | Supported | Supported | Xero UK |
| **Zoho Books** | Supported | Supported | Calculation requires manual webhook setup |
| **NetSuite** | Supported | Supported | Calculation requires plugin installation |

## E-commerce

| Platform | Syncing | Tax calculation | Notes |
|----------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| **Shopify** | Supported | Not supported | Order and transaction syncing |
| **WooCommerce** | Supported | Supported | Plugin supports both syncing and calculation |

## Payments

| Platform | Syncing | Tax calculation | Notes |
|----------|---------|-----------------|-------|
| **Stripe** | Supported | Not supported | Payment and subscription data |

## Banking

| Platform | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Plaid** | Bank account connection for balance and transaction data. Supports thousands of financial institutions. |

## Other integrations

| Platform | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| **DocuSign** | Electronic document signing |
| **Cal.com** | Meeting scheduling with advisors |
