Commenda is organized around three portals, each tailored to a different type of user.

## The three portals

### Company portal

The main platform for businesses. This is where company users:

- Manage entities (corporations, LLCs, etc.) across 100+ countries
- Track compliance obligations with an auto-generated compliance calendar
- Handle sales tax registrations, transactions, filings, and exemptions
- Document transfer pricing policies and benchmarking
- Browse and purchase services from the marketplace
- Manage documents, key persons, and corporate structure
- Connect accounting software and bank accounts

### Advisor portal

For accounting and advisory firms that serve company clients:

- View and manage assigned client entities
- Handle compliance work and update filing statuses
- Deliver services through service requests
- Manage team members and their client assignments
- Access research console and document templates
- Create proposals and audit reports

### Service provider portal

For agent firms that deliver specific services:

- Complete assigned service requests
- Manage client entities during service delivery
- Handle incorporation filings across supported countries
- Track SLA compliance and team workload

## Core workflow

A typical workflow on Commenda follows these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your entities">
    Add your business entities with their details, key persons, registrations, and documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track compliance">
    Commenda generates your compliance calendar automatically. Review deadlines and track filings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your data">
    Integrate with your accounting software to sync transaction data for tax and reporting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get work done">
    Handle filings yourself, or use the service marketplace to get professional help.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Stay organized">
    Monitor service requests, communicate with your advisors, and keep documents in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported jurisdictions

Commenda supports entity management and compliance tracking in 100+ countries. Incorporation services are available in:

- United States (all 50 states)
- Canada
- United Kingdom
- Ireland
- Singapore
- UAE
- India

Sales tax and indirect tax features support US multi-state compliance, EU VAT, and expanding international coverage.
