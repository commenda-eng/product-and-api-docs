This guide walks you through the essential steps to get up and running on Commenda.

## For company users

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.commenda.com](https://app.commenda.com) and sign up with your email address or Google account. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to confirm your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your company details">
    After signing in, you'll be guided through the onboarding flow. Enter your company name, basic details, and your profile information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your entities">
    Navigate to **Entities** in the sidebar and click **Add entity**. For each entity, provide the name, country of incorporation, entity type, and jurisdiction. Then add key details like EIN/tax ID, formation date, addresses, and registered agent information.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your corporate structure">
    If your entities have parent-subsidiary relationships, go to an entity's **Ownership** tab and add relationships to other entities in your company.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your integrations">
    Link your data sources — Shopify, Xero, QuickBooks, Stripe, or others. This imports your transaction data automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your compliance calendar">
    Commenda automatically generates compliance obligations based on your entities and their jurisdictions. Go to **Compliance Calendar** to see upcoming deadlines and track your filing status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If you have many entities to add, your Commenda account manager can help you bulk-import them.
</Tip>

## For advisor users

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your firm">
    Create your Advisor account and configure your firm profile, including your firm name, contact details, and service offerings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add clients">
    From the **Clients** section in your Advisor Portal, add your client businesses. You can invite clients directly or create their accounts on their behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign team members">
    Navigate to **Team Members** to add your team and assign them to specific client accounts. This controls who can view and manage each client's compliance data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor client compliance">
    Use your Advisor Portal dashboard to monitor compliance status, filing deadlines, and alerts across your entire client portfolio.
  </Step>
</Steps>
