Commenda has three types of users, each with their own portal and access level.

## Company users

Company users are the employees and team members of a business that uses Commenda to manage its entities and compliance. They access the main Commenda platform.

### Company roles

| Role | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Company Admin** | Full access to all company data, entities, and settings. Can manage team members and billing. |
| **Company User** | Full access to company data and entities. Cannot manage team members. |
| **Accountant** | Access to financial data across entities. Designed for external accountants and advisors. |
| **Controller** | Access limited to specific entities assigned to them. Useful for team members who manage a subset of your entities. |
| **Employee** | Limited access to their own payroll and equity information. |
| **Custom** | Permissions defined on a per-user basis. |

## Advisor users

Advisor users are members of accounting or advisory firms that serve companies through Commenda. They access the Advisor Portal, which provides:

- A view of all assigned client entities
- Ability to manage compliance filings and statuses
- Access to financial data for assigned clients
- Service request management and delivery
- Team management within their firm
- Research console and document templates

## Service provider users

Service provider users are members of agent firms that deliver specific services (incorporation, tax registration, registered agent, etc.) through the Commenda agent portal. They can:

- View and manage assigned client entities
- Complete tasks within service requests
- Track SLA compliance
- Manage their team and assignments
- Use tools like e-signatures and document templates

## Switching between views

If you have access to multiple roles (for example, you're both a company user and part of an advisor firm), you can switch between views using the account switcher in the sidebar.
