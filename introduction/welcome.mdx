## Welcome to Commenda

Commenda is an all-in-one platform that helps businesses stay compliant across jurisdictions. It centralizes indirect tax management, direct tax reporting, entity management, incorporation, and compliance monitoring — so you can manage obligations in the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, Ireland, Singapore, the United Arab Emirates, India, and more from a single place.

## Who is this documentation for?

This documentation covers the full Commenda platform and is organized for three audiences:

- **Businesses** managing their own tax compliance and corporate operations
- **Advisory firms** (Advisors) managing compliance on behalf of multiple clients
- **Service providers** handling the operational execution of compliance work
