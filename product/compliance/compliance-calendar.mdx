The **Compliance calendar** gives you a unified view of every filing obligation across all your entities and jurisdictions.

## Accessing the calendar

Click **Compliance Calendar** in the sidebar to open it.

## What you'll see

The calendar displays all compliance items organized by due date. For each item, you can see:

- **Entity name** — which entity the obligation belongs to
- **Filing type** — what needs to be filed (annual report, tax return, etc.)
- **Jurisdiction** — where the filing is due
- **Due date** — when it must be submitted
- **Status** — current progress (not filed, under filing, filed, etc.)
- **Assigned to** — who is responsible for the filing

## Filtering and sorting

You can filter the calendar by:

- **Entity** — view obligations for a specific entity
- **Status** — show only items that are not yet filed, or only completed items
- **Date range** — focus on a specific time period

## Taking action

From the compliance calendar, you can:

- **Update status** — mark a filing as under filing, filed, or completed
- **Create a service request** — get professional help with a filing by creating a service request
- **View details** — click any item to see its full details, history, and attached documents
- **Request an extension** — if available, file for an extension on the deadline

<Tip>
Check your compliance calendar weekly to stay ahead of upcoming deadlines. Items approaching their due date will be highlighted.
</Tip>
