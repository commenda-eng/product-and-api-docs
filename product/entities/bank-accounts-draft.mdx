Each entity in Commenda can have one or more bank accounts linked for financial data visibility and transaction tracking.

## Connecting a bank account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your entity">
    Navigate to **Entities** and select the entity whose bank account you want to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find bank account settings">
    Look for the **Bank accounts** section in your entity's details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add bank account">
    You'll be guided through Plaid's secure connection flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your bank">
    Search for your bank and sign in with your banking credentials. Plaid handles the authentication securely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose accounts">
    Select which accounts to connect (checking, savings, etc.).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get

Once connected, Commenda can access:

- **Account balances** — current balance information
- **Transactions** — transaction history for reconciliation and tax purposes
- **Account details** — account type and institution information

## Managing bank accounts

From an entity's bank accounts section, you can:

- **Set a primary account** — designate one account as the primary for the entity
- **View account details** — see account type, balance, and institution
- **Remove an account** — disconnect a bank account from the entity

## Manual entry

If your bank isn't available through Plaid, you can manually enter account details:

- Bank name
- Account number
- Routing number (for US accounts)
- IFSC code (for Indian accounts)
- Account type (checking, savings, current)

## Security

Bank connections through Plaid use bank-level encryption. Commenda never stores your banking login credentials. You can disconnect your account at any time.

<Info>
Plaid supports thousands of financial institutions. If your bank isn't available, manual entry is always an option.
</Info>
