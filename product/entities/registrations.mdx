The **Registrations** tab tracks where each entity is registered with government authorities and the status of each registration.

## Types of registrations

- **Tax registrations** — sales tax, income tax, payroll tax, VAT
- **Business registrations** — state registrations, business licenses
- **Employer registrations** — EIN, employer accounts
- **Other government IDs** — DUNS number, NAICS code, etc.

## Viewing registrations

1. Go to **Entities** and select an entity.
2. Click the **Registrations** tab.

Each registration shows:
- The authority and jurisdiction
- Registration number or ID
- Status (pending, active, inactive)
- Filing frequency (if applicable)

## Adding a registration

Click **Add registration** and provide the registration details. You can upload supporting documents (registration certificates, confirmation letters) directly to the registration record.

## Credentials

For registrations that require login credentials (e.g., state tax portal accounts), Commenda securely stores these credentials. Only authorized team members can view or use them.

<Warning>
Credentials are encrypted and stored securely. Never share login credentials outside of Commenda's secure credential storage.
</Warning>
