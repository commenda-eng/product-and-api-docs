Commenda integrates with DocuSign to let you collect electronic signatures on documents directly from the platform.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload or generate a document">
    Start with a document that needs signatures — either upload your own or generate one from a template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send for signature">
    Click **Send for signature** and select the signers. Enter their name and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track status">
    Monitor the signing status from within Commenda. You'll see when the document has been sent, viewed, and signed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the signed document">
    Once all parties have signed, the completed document is automatically stored in your Commenda account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where e-signatures are used

E-signatures are most commonly used within service requests. When a service provider needs a document signed:

1. They attach the document to a service request task.
2. The task is assigned to the signer.
3. The signer receives a notification and completes the signature through DocuSign.
4. The signed document is stored in the service request.

## Signer experience

When someone receives a document to sign:

- They receive an email notification from DocuSign
- They can review the document and sign electronically
- No DocuSign account is required to sign
- The signed document is available to all parties

<Info>
The DocuSign integration is available to service providers and advisors. Contact your Commenda account manager for setup.
</Info>
